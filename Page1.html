Orange Cats (in General)

The Orange cat stereotype is that they are all silly little cats and always do the silliest known stuff known to man-kind.


Himalayan Persian Cats

Himalayan Persian Cats are very peaceful when introduced nicely to a new person. They love to play, cuddle and be explorers. These cats are intelligent.


Munchkin Cats

Munchkin Cats have very short legs, because of that they cannot jump very high. These cats are short and adorable. They are loving, affectionate and playful.


Chartreux Cats

These cats are great with children because of their calm & gentle behavior. Chartreux cats are quiet, observant & intelligent. Originated in France.


Sphynx Cats

Sphynx cats are hairless cats that origionated in Canada. They are very extroverted, curious, energetic and cuddly. They can learn tricks easily because of how smart they are.
